History of the Company

TVR is an independent British manufacturer of high-end sports cars. The company lightweight sports cars with powerful engines and was, at one time, the third-largest specialised sports car manufacturer in the world, offering a diverse range of coup√©s and convertibles. The abbreviation TVR stems from the name of the company's owner Trevor Wilkinson, his first garage sporting the letters T, V and R. The history of TVR can be divided into several eras, each of which is associated with the company's owner at the start of that period:

From 1946 Until 1965, founder Trevor Wilkinson, who left in 1962

From 1965 Until 1981, Martin Lilley

From 1981 Until 2004, Peter Wheeler

From 2004 Until 2013, Nikolay Smolensky

From 2013 Until present, syndicate of British businessmen led by Les Edgar
